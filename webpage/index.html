About Singh Sabha Gurudwara Berlin

Singh Sabha Gurudwara Berlin e.V. has been serving the Sikh community and all visitors since 2005. Our Gurudwara is not just a place of worship but a community center that promotes Sikh values, culture, and traditions.

We are committed to:

  • Providing spiritual guidance through regular programs
  • Education through Punjabi classes and religious studies
  • Community service through Langar and other seva activities
  • Cultural preservation and promotion
