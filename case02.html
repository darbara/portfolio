Overview

A massive market — stuck offline.

StoreHub is a leading business management platform in SEA. Merchans had digitised inventory, but none were selling online. The infrastructure existed, but the barrier was perception and friction.

My Role Lead Designer
Markets Malaysia · Thailand · SG · PH
Timeline 0 → 1 Product Launch
Impact 13K+ Active Merchants
Main interface of the newly designed marketplace mobile experience.
The Problem

"Going online feels like starting over."

The mental model of "uploading products" triggered dread. Merchants trusted StoreHub for in-store usage, but that didn't automatically extend to the web.

Insight 01
Catalogue Anxiety
Merchants with 100+ SKUs feared the data entry effort required.
Insight 02
Identity Gap
Owners didn't see themselves as "online sellers" — an identity barrier.
Design Strategy

Shrink the leap, not the ambition.

Pillar 01

Micro-decisions

Designed the onboarding to feel like one small, confident step rather than a massive leap into unknown territory by reducing choice paralysis.

Pillar 02

Data Leverage

Used existing POS inventory as the foundation for the online store, removing the "start from zero" hurdle for thousands of active merchants.

A one-click digitalisation flow that automatically ports POS inventory to the web storefront.
Impact

Thousands of retailers reached.
